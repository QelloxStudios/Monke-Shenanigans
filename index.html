🎮 About

Monke Shenanigans is a fast-paced VR movement game inspired by gorilla-style locomotion. Swing, climb, launch yourself around chaotic maps, and master movement-based gameplay.

✨ Features

  • Physics-based VR movement
  • Movement-focused gameplay
  • Parkour objectives
  • Custom monkey player model
  • Large maps with trees and movement routes
  • Multiplayer
